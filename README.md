<h1>Austin</h1>

<p>I'm a theoretical physicist at the 
University of Cambridge. Over the years, I've worked on many different aspects of physics, including quantum field theory, general relativity, cosmology, and condensed matter physics. 


<br><br>
You can read more about my 
research and publications on my <a href="research.html"><u>research 
pages</u></a>. You can find lecture notes on various topics in 
theoretical physics on my <a href="teaching.html"><u>teaching 
pages</u></a>. </p>
